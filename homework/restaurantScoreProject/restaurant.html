San Francisco Restaurants Risk Levels

By Mengyue Sun

The map below presents San Francisco restaurants inspection scores conducted by the SF Health Department from 2016 to 2018. Inspection scores are based on violations, which was divided into three categories:

  1.   Low risk: violations that are low risk or have no immediate risk to the public health and safety
  2.   Moderate risk: violations that are of a moderate risk to the public health and safety
  3.   High risk: violations that directly relate to the transmission of food borne illnesses
