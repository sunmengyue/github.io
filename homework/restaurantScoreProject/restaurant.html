San Francisco Restaurants Inspection Scores

By Mengyue Sun

The map below presents San Francisco restaurants inspection scores conducted by the SF Health Department in 2016. Inspection scores are based on violations, which was divided into three categories:

  1. High risk: violations that directly relate to the transmission of food borne illnesses
  2. Moderate risk: violations that are of a moderate risk to the public health and safety
  3. Low risk: violations that are low risk or have no immediate risk to the public health and safety
